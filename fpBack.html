Back Workout

Inverted row uses a horizontal pull to target the lats, traps, rhomboids, delts, and biceps.

straight-arm pulldown is an exercise that primarily targets the upper back muscles. It also works the upper arms and shoulders.

Deadlift is a compound exercise that targets the muscles of the back, shoulders, and legs.

Superman This move targets your lower back (erector spinae muscles), abs, glutes, hamstrings, and upper back.

seated row targets the muscles in your upper back and also the latissimus dorsi a muscle on the outer side of the chest wall.
