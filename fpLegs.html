Legs Workout

Back squats target the posterior chain or the back of your body including the lower back, glutes, and hamstrings. The quads and core are also engaged

Calf raises can help you build larger calf muscles. Calf raises target the muscles on the back of your lower legs.

Leg extensions focus the tension on just your quadriceps. This is the only leg exercise that targets your quadriceps without involving other muscles

Sumo squats target the lower body muscles, including the glutes, quads, calves and hamstrings. Due to the wide stance position, sumo squats also target the adductor muscles.

Reverse lunge will fire up your core, it's primarily a lower body-strengthening exercise that targets the quads and glutes while also engaging a handful of other muscles below the waist.
