Dumbbells

a bar with weights at the ends that is usually used for exercise or building strength. One of the most significant benefits of exercising with dumbbells is that it helps to build strength. Dumbbells are versatile and can be used to target a wide range of muscle groups. By challenging your muscles, you can increase your strength and improve your overall fitness level.
