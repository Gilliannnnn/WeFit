Core Workout

Planks target the core (transversus abdominis, rectus abdominis, and oblique muscles),(deltoids),(lats, triceps, traps, chest, biceps, rhomboids),(glutes, quads, hamstrings, calves, extensor muscles in feet)

Dead bug exercise works every muscle in the core.

Leg raises work muscle groups throughout your whole body, including the rectus abdominis, hip flexor muscles, hamstrings, and lower back muscles.

Bicycle crunches are well-rounded exercises that target your entire core.

Russian twists target the following muscles: obliques. rectus abdominis. transverse abdominis.
