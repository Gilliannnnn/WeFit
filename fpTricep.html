Tricep Workout

Skull crushers mainly target your triceps muscles, but they don't stop there. “By isolating and extending, the exercise strengthens the triceps.

Triceps Kick Back targeting your triceps brachii muscle. Triceps kickbacks work the lateral head, medial head, and long head of the muscle, putting special emphasis on the lateral head.

Bench presses work your arms, shoulders, and chest, targeting the same primary muscle groups: Pectoralis major. Anterior deltoids. Triceps brachii.

Tricep Dips your major pectoralis and minor pectoralis muscles benefit the most from the chest dip, however, the anterior deltoid, triceps, rhomboids, levator scapulae, and latissimus dorsi are all engaged as well.

Up down planks primarily target your core muscles, including your abs, obliques, and lower back. They also engage your shoulders, chest, and triceps.
