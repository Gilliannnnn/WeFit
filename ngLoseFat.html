Loose Fat

Get Plenty of Sleep

-Your body's appetite hormones may be affected by sleep deprivation. increasing your propensity to consume and accumulate fat. Aim to get 7-9 hours of restful sleep every night can help in weight loss. Sleep also helps in muscle recovery.

Incorporate Strength Training

-Building lean muscular mass with strength training helps increase metabolism and aids in fat-burning even when you're at rest. resistance band exercises or bodyweight exercises, at least 3-4 times a week to start seeing results.

Incorporate High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

-HIIT workouts alternate between intense bursts of activity and short rest periods, According to studies, HIIT can enhance cardiovascular health and boost fat burning.
