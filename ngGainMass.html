Gain Mass

Prioritize Protein-Rich Foods

-Protein is key for muscle growth. Lean meats, seafood, eggs, dairy products, and plant-based choices such as tofu and beans at every meal. this aims for a daily intake of 1 gram of protein per pound of body weight.

Eat More Calories than You Burn

-You must have excess calorie intake to gain mass. this means you should consume more calories than what your body can burn every day, Eat nutrient-dense foods to fuel your body and maximize gains.

Stay Consistent with Your Workouts

-To build muscle, you must be consistent. you must at least 3-4 times a week, focus on different variety of muscular groups. To make sure you're efficiently addressing all areas, stick to a program that is structured.
